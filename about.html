Who We Are

More Than a Financial Institution

Prime Ledger Group was founded with a clear purpose: to provide the African continent's businesses, governments, and institutions with financial and operational services of the highest international standard.

We are a multidisciplinary firm offering project management, financial services, procurement, and ethical mineral mandate advisory. Our approach is grounded in rigorous analysis, ethical practice, and a deep understanding of the African business environment.

From our base in Nairobi, Kenya, we serve clients across East, West, Central, and Southern Africa — bringing world-class solutions to markets that deserve nothing less.
